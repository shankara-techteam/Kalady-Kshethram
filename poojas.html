⭐ Masa Shivaratri Seva

Sri Dhakshinamurthy Homam

With the divine blessings of His Holiness Jagadguru Sri Sri Bharathi Theertha Mahasannidhanam and Jagadguru Sri Sri Vidhushekhara Bharathi Sannidhanam.

Sri Dhakshinamurthy Homam is performed on every Masa Shivaratri for students, seeking the blessings of Vidyabhivruddhi (Educational Excellence) and Ayur Arogyam (Health and Well-being).

Seva Offering Cost ₹3,000.00 (Per Homam) Three Thousand Rupees Only

Other Traditional Sevas

Devotees can sponsor several special prayers and services to support the preservation of our spiritual heritage.

Adi Shankara Ashtothara Archana

₹150

Sponsor the worship of Sri Adi Shankaracharya, performed by chanting his 108 sacred names. This seva invokes blessings of clarity, wisdom, and spiritual progress.

Daily Seva

Sharadamba Suhasini Puja

₹500

Special worship dedicated to Goddess Sharadamba, the embodiment of wisdom, fine arts, and speech. Performed on Fridays, this puja brings blessings of learning, intellect, and positive domestic energy.

Weekly (Fridays)

Annadanam Seva

₹1,008

Sponsor a full day of traditional vegetarian meals served to pilgrims, sannyasis, and visitors at the Sringeri Math dining hall in Kalady. Providing food is considered one of the highest forms of charity.

Daily Offering

Veda Pathashala Support

Any Amount

Contribute towards the traditional boarding, lodging, healthcare, and education expenses of young Vedic students residing and studying at the Kalady Veda Pathashala.
