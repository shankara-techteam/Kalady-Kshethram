## Sri Dhakshinamurthy Homam (Masa Shivaratri Seva) — ₹3,000/Homam
Performed every Masa Shivaratri for students, seeking Vidyabhivruddhi (educational excellence) and Ayur
Arogyam (health/well-being). Conducted by traditional Vedic priests invoking Lord Dhakshinamurthy.

**Scheduled Masa Shivaratri Homam dates (2026–2027):** 15 Apr 2026 (Wed), 15 May 2026 (Fri), 13 Jun 2026

07 Dec 2026 (Mon), 05 Jan 2027 (Tue), 04 Feb 2027 (Thu), 06 Mar 2027 (Sat), 05 Apr 2027 (Mon).

## Other Sevas
- **Adi Shankara Ashtothara Archana** — ₹150. Daily. Chanting 108 names of Sri Adi Shankaracharya.
- **Sharadamba Suhasini Puja** — ₹500. Weekly (Fridays). Worship of Goddess Sharadamba for wisdom/learning.
- **Annadanam Seva** — ₹1,008. Daily. Sponsors a full day of vegetarian meals for pilgrims and sannyasis.
- **Veda Pathashala Support** — Any amount. Supports boarding, lodging, healthcare, education of Vedic
  students at Kalady Veda Pathashala.
