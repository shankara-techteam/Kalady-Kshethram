# Visiting Information
Sources: Sringeri.net – Official Kalady Branch Page (location/distances, authoritative); Official Website
Home Page (timings, accommodation)

## Temple Timings
- Morning: 5:30 AM – 12:30 PM
- Evening: 4:30 PM – 8:30 PM

## Location
Kalady, Ernakulam District, Kerala — PIN 683574. A village accessible by road and rail, alighting at
Angamaly. Kochi International Airport (Nedumbassery) is nearby.

## Travel Distances (per Sringeri.net, authoritative)
- Kalady Bus Stand: ~1 km
- Angamaly Railway Station: ~7 km
- Aluva Railway Station: ~22 km
- Cochin International Airport: ~7 km
- Cochin Port Trust: ~35 km

## Guest Accommodation (Adi Shankara Nilayam / Sri Sringeri Shankara Math Guest House)
- Single/double rooms with standard amenities.
- Traditional vegetarian meals (Annadanam) served daily.
- Library with books on Advaita philosophy and Sringeri Math history.
- **Booking:** first-come, first-served due to limited availability. Contact the Kalady branch office (see
  Contact information) at least 7–10 days in advance.

## Notes for Pilgrims
- The Kshethram is a place of worship and quiet reflection; visitors should maintain decorum.
- Pilgrims and tourists visit especially during Sri Shankara Jayanthi and Sarada Navarathri festivals, the
  temple's two largest annual draws.
- For special event dates, confirm with the temple office or official website, as exact dates vary yearly.
